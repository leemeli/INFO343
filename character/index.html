Squidward Tentacles

Squidward Tentacles is one of the main characters in the children's animated show Spongebob Squarepants. He works at the Krusty Krab as a cashier and is closely acquainted with Spongebob and Patrick. More information about Squidward can be found here at Wikipedia.

I admire Squidward because he always has to deal with Spongebob and Patrick's bullcrap and I feel like I relate to that deeply on a personal level. He isn't fakely nice to anybody and is blunt about how he feels.
